# ⚠️ Common Cyber Threats (Especially for Teens)

## 1. **Phishing**
Fake messages designed to trick you into giving:
- Passwords
- OTPs
- Personal info

Clues:
- Urgency ("Your account will be deleted!")
- Unknown links
- Suspicious emails

## 2. **Scams**
Online shopping scams, fake giveaways, "too good to be true" deals.

## 3. **Malware**
Software that harms your device:
- Viruses  
- Spyware  
- Keyloggers  

## 4. **Social Engineering**
Hackers target **people**, not just computers.
They pretend to be:
- Friends
- Teachers  
- Delivery services  

## 5. **Account Hijacking**
Weak passwords → attacker breaks in.
